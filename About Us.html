K - Tech College



K-Tech College is an institute that offers higher education in applied sciences, mainly in computer science. With the ever-changing world, computer science has become the most promising field of study. Hence, a group of scholars came together to form this institute hoping to increase the chances of our Sri Lankan students in local and international platforms.


We offer the courses listed down below.

  • BSc Hons in Applied Physics
  • BSc Hons in Chemical Technology
  • BSc Hons in Computer Science and Technology
  • BSc Hons in Computing & Information Systems
  • BSc Hons in Data Science
  • BSc Hons in Information Systems
  • BSc Hons in Software Engineering
  • BSc in Physical Science
* Please contact us for further information.

ICTC


The Information and Communication Technology Commission (ICTC) of Sri Lanka is the apex ICT institution of the Government. Currently, we are a high-level entity providing National Level Guidance and Coordination for National Priorities. This includes offering efficient and citizen-centric services, simplifying market processes and state mechanisms, expanding Digital Governance through Information Technology as a knowledge exchange tool, establishing international e-commerce and e-payment systems, and designing new laws and organizational frameworks for Data Protection, Cyber Security, and Intellectual Property Rights.


Why involved in AWAC?

  • It's our vision for Sri Lanka to be Digitally Inclusive and Prosperous.
  • To improve the tendency of the young to pursue their higher studies in computer science.
  • We want to empower the young generation with the right tools.

“AWAC Workshop” is just a part of the well-known national project, "Tomorrow"; born from the idea to empower the young with the necessary skillset to conquer the world. Tomorrow is a project ranging in almost all fields of studies, including vocational education. From all the mini-projects, AWAC is the youngest member of Tomorrow. It is under the surveillance of ICTC, and conducted through K-Tech College. The main objective of AWAC is to empower future programmers with the knowledge and skills required for programming. It is done in a way the young generation can enjoy learning programming, inspire them to experiment with coding, and encourage innovation.

What You Can Look Forward to;

  • 2 lectures by resource persons per day (A morning lecture and an evening lecture)
  • Grouped according to the excellence level for an efficient outcome

    • These groups receive separate mentors from K-Tech College and other higher education institutes.
    The mentors will map a customized training method to each group and help the participants.

    Beginner
    - Programming fundamentals
    - How to approach a problem and get the desired solution
    - Basic coding techniques
    Intermediate
    - Advanced coding techniques
    - Problem set to solve
    - Guided programming
    Advanced
    - Problem set to solve
    - Unguided programming
    - Shortcuts and time-managing tips
  • Fun games (To improve the coding skills)
  • Saturday night camp by the bonfire
